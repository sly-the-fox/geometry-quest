# Geometry Quest: Codex of Resonance
## Complete Game Design Document

---

## **Core Game Concept**

**Genre:** Action-Adventure RPG (Zelda-style)  
**Theme:** Sacred geometry meets energy work in an immersive mystical world  
**Core Mechanic:** Transform geometric processes into magical weapons and abilities to solve dungeons and defeat bosses

---

## **Story & World Setting**

### **The Shattered Codex**
Long ago, the **Codex Universalis** maintained harmony across all realms through geometric resonance. When the Codex was shattered by the **Shadow Architect**, reality became fractured into discordant patterns. As **The Resonance Keeper**, you must restore the scattered geometric processes to heal the world.

### **The World of Harmonia**
- **16 Resonance Regions** - Each corresponds to a resonance type (Icosahedral Torus, Collapsing Triangle, etc.)
- **Sacred Geometric Temples** - Ancient structures that house the lost geometric processes
- **Field Corruption Zones** - Areas where shadow patterns have taken hold
- **The Crystal Nexus** - Central hub world connecting all regions

---

## **Character & Progression System**

### **The Resonance Keeper (Player Character)**
- Starts with basic **Triangle Flow** ability
- Gains **Resonance Type Affinity** through discovery (player can specialize in 1-3 types)
- **Field Integrity** replaces traditional health (restored through geometric harmony)
- **Geometric Mastery** levels unlock advanced weapon combinations

### **Character Stats**
- **Coherence** - Affects accuracy and energy efficiency
- **Resonance** - Determines which geometric frequencies you can access
- **Stability** - Your resistance to field corruption
- **Flow** - Movement speed and dodge capability

## **Weapons & Tools System**

### **Primary Geometric Weapons**

#### **1. Tetrahedron Blade (3-Sided)**
- **Type:** Sword
- **Power:** Mobile Awareness - Cuts through rigid patterns
- **Special:** Phases through shields, rotates to attack from any angle
- **Upgrade Path:** Crystalline Tetrahedron → Merkaba Dual-Blade

#### **2. Pentagram Inverter (5-Sided)**
- **Type:** Mystical Staff
- **Power:** Phase Inversion - Breaks duality loops and stuck patterns
- **Special:** Reflects enemy attacks back inverted, dissolves shadow barriers
- **Boss Effectiveness:** Destroys pattern-locked bosses

#### **3. Hexagonal Shield (6-Sided)**
- **Type:** Defensive Tool
- **Power:** Crystalline Stabilization - Creates protective barriers
- **Special:** Absorbs energy attacks and converts them to healing
- **Environmental Use:** Stabilizes unstable platforms, calms chaotic areas

#### **4. Heptagon Lens (7-Sided)**
- **Type:** Observation Tool (Detective Mode)
- **Power:** Suspension Threshold - Reveals hidden patterns
- **Special:** Slows time, shows enemy weak points, reveals secret passages
- **Puzzle Use:** Witnesses pattern sequences, identifies correct solutions

#### **5. Octagon Harmonizer (8-Sided)**
- **Type:** Completion Tool
- **Power:** Octave Closure - Completes cycles and integrates energy
- **Special:** Finishes partial mechanisms, heals field corruption
- **Boss Use:** Seals defeated bosses, completes environmental puzzles

#### **6. Nonagon Absorber (9-Sided)**
- **Type:** Containment Device
- **Power:** Recursive Absorption - Contains emotional overflow and thought loops
- **Special:** Creates vortex traps, absorbs enemy attacks indefinitely
- **Environmental Use:** Clears emotional residue from corrupted areas

#### **7. Decagon Resetter (10-Sided)**
- **Type:** Reset Tool
- **Power:** Harmonic Reset - Clears field memory and resets patterns
- **Special:** Returns environments to original state, removes all buffs/debuffs
- **Boss Use:** Resets boss phases, clears accumulated damage patterns

#### **8. Hendecagon Disruptor (11-Sided)**
- **Type:** Prime Disruption Weapon
- **Power:** Pattern Liberation - Breaks entrenched behaviors and stagnation
- **Special:** Causes enemies to break their own patterns, liberates trapped NPCs
- **Environmental Use:** Breaks ancient seals, disrupts stuck mechanisms

#### **9. Dodecagon Crown (12-Sided)**
- **Type:** Legendary Headpiece
- **Power:** Harmonic Mastery - Multidimensional alignment
- **Special:** Allows use of multiple geometric weapons simultaneously

#### **10. Tridecagon Catalyst (13-Sided)**
- **Type:** Evolution Weapon
- **Power:** Dimensional Expansion - Consciousness evolution and growth patterns
- **Special:** Opens new dimensional pathways, evolves basic enemies into allies
- **Environmental Use:** Expands puzzle solutions, creates new possibilities

#### **11. Tetradecagon Harmonizer (14-Sided)**
- **Type:** Dual Stabilizer
- **Power:** Bifurcated Field Balance - Harmonizes masculine/feminine, light/shadow
- **Special:** Balances opposing forces without destroying either
- **Boss Use:** Resolves internal conflicts in bosses, creates perfect equilibrium

### **Advanced Weapons (Late Game)**

#### **12. Merkaba Wings**
- **Type:** Flight/Transportation Tool
- **Power:** Rotational Polarity Integration
- **Special:** Flight, dimensional travel between resonance regions

#### **13. Toroidal Field Generator**
- **Type:** Ultimate Weapon
- **Power:** Self-Sustaining Dynamic Flow
- **Special:** Creates renewable energy, massive area healing, final boss weapon

### **Ranged Weapons**

#### **Frequency Bow**
- **Arrows:** Different pyramid-sided arrow heads (3-14 sides)
- **3-Sided Arrows:** Pierce through multiple enemies
- **5-Sided Arrows:** Invert enemy AI (makes them help you temporarily)
- **9-Sided Arrows:** Create recursive loops that pull enemies into whirlpools
- **11-Sided Arrows:** Disrupt boss patterns and create openings

#### **Resonance Boomerang**
- **Form:** Vesica Piscis shape (infinite loop)
- **Power:** Returns with collected energy from what it hits
- **Upgrade:** Lemniscate Boomerang (figure-8 path, hits twice)

##### **Pre-Boss: The Guardian Trials**
- **16 Resonance Guardians:** Each tests mastery of their specific type

- **Integration Challenge:** Combine all abilities in creative synthesis

##### **Final Boss Phase 1: The Fragmented Architect**
- **Appearance:** Massive geometric entity composed of all corrupted patterns
- **Mechanics:** 
  - **Structured Prison Form (4-sided):** Creates limitation fields, attacks with rigid patterns
  - **Must use** Pentagram Inverter to break its duality structures

- **Appearance:** Swirling vortex of disconnected geometric fragments

  - **Must use** Hexagonal Shield to stabilize self, then Nonagon Absorber to contain the chaos

- **Appearance:** Defeated entity lying in geometric fragments
